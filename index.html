About Me

My name is Brandon Williams and I am an aspiring Full Stack web developer. My overall goal, is always to produce work that both meets an exceeds what a client is looking for. I have over 7 years of education in the areas of I.T. Networking and Security, and have been exposed to, and learning coding and web development since my days in middle school. In the late days of 2019, I decided it was time for a change. Having felt stuck in a rut for quite some time, and feeling less than satisfied with where my life seemed to be heading, I decided to shift all of my focus into learning Full Stack web development, where I felt I would have the best chance at finding satisfaction, and letting my creative talents truly come to the surface. To this end after much searching, I enrolled in the University of Wisconsin Coding Bootcamp, and with renewed passion and enthusiasm started down the path of yet another exciting adventure in life. When I am not coding, or in the process of delving into learning about a new and interesting topic, I can be found sitting at my piano, reading a book, or by his royal decree, (I just pay the rent) showering attention and the more than occasional belly rub on my cat Peanut.
